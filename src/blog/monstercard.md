I had this thought this morning. What if I make a tiny change towards YGO's 
monster cards? Making how they work similar to card games (MTG). What if 
attacking a monster with higher defense reduce their defense until you can 
destroy them in battle? It's fun to think how this small change will change 
how YGO plays. I'm planning to make a simple prototype with YGO Ultimate 
Masters 2006 as the base. Of course, moving on from a simple prototype will be 
a major undertaking.
